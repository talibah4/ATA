A Sand Grain’s Dream

Take a journey into a short film that I wrote, directed, & produced. It tells the story of a sand grain’s dream, via the embodiment of a child.

The film is currently streaming in festivals nationally & internationally!

Katra Film Series, NY NY

On November 8th, my short film, A Sand Grain’s Dream, screened at Katra Film Series. Katra Film Series spotlights new talent & serves as a launching pad for short content creators. Every month Katra showcases a selection of quality works by emerging artists from all over the world in front of a live audience in NYC.
